About Rubra

Rubra is an independent technology company focused on building modern, intuitive applications for individuals, teams, and businesses.

We care deeply about clear design, reliable performance, and user-friendly experiences. Our goal is to make powerful tools feel simple for everyone, whether they are using a smartphone for the first time or managing a growing organisation.
