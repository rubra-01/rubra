About WorkLink

WorkLink is a modern work platform that helps daily wage workers and employers connect around nearby opportunities through one focused, easy-to-use app.

Built and maintained by Rubra in India.

For workers

WorkLink is designed for daily wage workers and skilled tradespeople who want to find nearby opportunities without complexity. You can create a simple profile, highlight your skills, and stay visible to employers who are actively looking for reliable workers.

  • Create a profile with your skills, description, and location
  • Discover local work posted by nearby employers
  • See clear work details before deciding to reach out
  • Stay in control of how and when employers can contact you

For employers

Employers — including homeowners, small businesses, and contractors — can use WorkLink to quickly reach nearby workers. The focus is on making it easy to find the right person for the work without middlemen or complicated processes.

  • Post clear work requirements for local tasks and projects
  • Browse worker profiles with skills, descriptions, and locations
  • Reach workers directly to discuss work and availability
  • Build ongoing relationships with trusted local workers

How WorkLink fits into Rubra

WorkLink is the first product from Rubra, built around a clear idea: connect workers with local opportunities in a simple, trustworthy way. It focuses on people in Kashmir but is designed so the same experience can work in many regions.

The app is guided by values such as trust, accessibility, community, and empowerment. It is part of a wider ecosystem of products from Rubra that are built to feel simple and dependable.
